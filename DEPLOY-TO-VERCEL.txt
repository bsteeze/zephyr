Harmony of the Year — Vercel package

Files in this package:
- index.html
- harmony.css
- harmony.js

Deploy:
1. In Vercel, open the zephyr project.
2. Create a new deployment by uploading these three files together, or upload this extracted folder.
3. Confirm index.html is at the top level—not inside another nested folder.
4. Deploy to Production.
5. Under Project Settings > Domains, attach zephyr.guru and www.zephyr.guru.

No PHP runtime is required. All saved groups remain in the visitor's browser localStorage.
